# **App Name**: Lucro Express

## Core Features:

- Immersive Product Showcase: Engaging sections to highlight the 'Marketplace Renda Express' benefits, course modules, and unique access to the exclusive expert guide, using compelling visuals and descriptive text.
- Expert Credibility & Testimonials: Dedicated area to feature the 'exclusive guide' expert, showcasing their background and success, alongside authentic user testimonials to build trust and social proof.
- Key Benefits & Tools Overview: Clear presentation of how the 'controle e gestão das suas vendas' tools and guide lead to 'renda extra ou principal' and 'prioridade no seu lucro' on Facebook Marketplace, including mockups or explanations of tool features.
- Pricing & Purchase Call-to-Action: A prominent and persuasive section guiding users through package options and a clear call-to-action button for purchasing access to the infoproduct.
- FAQ & Support Section: An organized section answering frequently asked questions about the infoproduct, its content, and access, alongside options for further support.
- AI-Powered Sales Navigator Tool: A conversational AI chatbot tool embedded on the sales page, providing instant, intelligent answers to prospective buyers' questions about the course content, benefits, and how to utilize the marketplace management strategies outlined in the infoproduct.

## Style Guidelines:

- Background color: A deep, desaturated blue-purple (#21222C), providing a sophisticated and professional backdrop that promotes focus on content.
- Primary color: A vibrant yet authoritative blue-purple (#4763EB) for headlines and key interactive elements, signifying innovation and trust.
- Accent color: A lighter, clear blue (#85C2E0) used sparingly for call-to-action highlights, interactive components, and visual emphasis, providing contrast and guiding user attention.
- Headline font: 'Space Grotesk' (sans-serif), for a modern, slightly technical feel that aligns with online marketplaces and digital tools.
- Body font: 'Inter' (sans-serif), chosen for its readability, neutrality, and clean appearance, ensuring clarity in content delivery.
- Use clean, minimalist line icons that represent concepts of growth, profit, data, and social media platforms, maintaining a professional and contemporary aesthetic.
- A modern, spacious, and responsive layout featuring clear content blocks and hierarchical information presentation, optimized for legibility and engagement across all devices.
- Subtle and elegant micro-interactions and transitions on scroll and hover, used to highlight important information and enhance the overall user experience without distracting from the core sales message.
